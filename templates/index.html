Introduction

If you love watching or playing the "Jeopardy" game, this app is for you! This app was created to showcase natural language processing in a fun and imaginative way, using a dataset consisting of the vast majority of Jeopardy questions aired from season 1 to season 39.

Our current functionality includes:

Wordclouds
Explore "Wordclouds" to view some of the NLP visualizations we have created from the dataset!

Learn Mode
Explore "Learn Mode" tab to learn about Jeopardy questions aired so far, with custom NLP classified categories and clue values!

Game Mode
Play a game on "Game Mode" tab and test your Jeopardy knowledge against our NLP model selected questions, complete with cosine similarity grading!
